CRIMINAL REVISION APPLICATION NO.634 OF 2017
Sudhakar Gopal Pawar
Vs. M.K. Kocharekar i/b R.A. Kale for the Applicant
Mr. Vinod Chate, APP, for the Respondent - State

CORAM: Mrs. In this revision application, the applicant/accused is prosecuted under sections 302, 397 of the
Indian Penal Code r/w sections 37(1)(a) and 135 of the Mumbai Police Act in Sessions Case No.701
of 2011. They
sold the gold ornaments and obtained money. Per contra, learned Prosecutor has argued that this is a murder and robbery case and the
applicant is prosecuted in this case as he is the main person who has with help from other accused,
had sold the gold ornaments which they have taken from the jewellery shop. At the outset, the portion and the statement relied on by the learned Prosecutor in the
memorandum recorded under section 27, is inadmissible. Hence, the order of the trial Court dated 27.9.2011 is hereby quashed and set aside. (MRIDULA BHATKAR, J.)
