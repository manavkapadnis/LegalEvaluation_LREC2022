Sambhaji Namdeo Sawant on 25 July, 2019

Bombay High Court
Shri. CORAM : M.S.KARNIK, J.

DATE : 25th JULY, 2019 P.C. :
. Heard learned Counsel for the appellant and learned Counsel for the respondent. The defendant alleged that in January 2008, he had harvested corn crop in
the land possessed by him out of survey No.211/1A. It is alleged that the plaintiff committed theft of
the crop and complaint therefore came to be lodged to the police station on the basis of which the
appellant filed Regular Criminal Case No. The appellant preferred Appeal against the judgment
and decree of the trial Court which came to be dismissed by First Appellate Court.
